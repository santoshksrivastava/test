About NYAAY SETU

Police department is main agency which interacts with all agencies i.e Prosecution, Forensic, Court and Jail. Police department should have complete data all the pillars of ICJS on CCTNS.

NYAAY SETU is developed on the top of CCTNS by using APIs of various pillars of ICJS i.e Prosecution, Forensic, Court and Prison. Patches added in Crime and Criminal Tracking Network & Systems (CCTNS) to integrate it with eProsecution and eFSL for seamless flow of information between CCTNS, prosecution, and FSL. of newly enacted criminal laws and is intended to provide a holistic solution in a contributory manner to all the stakeholders looking for such type of service.
